# The Local Business Automation Pack — $49 (intro $29)

5 plug-and-play automations that stop local service businesses from leaking
leads, calls, bookings, and reviews. Import, plug in your keys, done.

**Who it's for:** owners + operators of local service businesses (trades,
clinics, salons, gyms, cleaners) and the people who set them up.

**What's inside (`templates/`):**
1. **Missed-Call Text-Back** — a missed call auto-texts the caller in seconds. *(flagship workflow included as importable n8n JSON)*
2. **Speed-to-Lead Router** — new web-form leads get an instant text + email + booking link.
3. **Auto Review Requests** — job marked done → review request with one-tap link.
4. **Appointment Reminders** — 24h + 1h reminders with one-tap reschedule (kills no-shows).
5. **Lead-Nurture Sequence** — 5-touch follow-up for leads that don't convert yet.

**Build recipes + step-by-step setup:** `templates/SETUP.md`.

**Cost model:** built in **n8n (free, self-host)**. The *client* pays their own
Twilio/SMS usage — **$0 to you**.

## How this monetizes (stack)

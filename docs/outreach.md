# Service Outreach Kit — first-revenue engine

Goal: book 1–2 paid "Missed-Call Money Recovery" builds ($300–$1,500) in
week 2 — **no audience required**. Owner runs this; everything below is ready
to paste.

## Target list (free, 15 min/day)
Google Maps → search e.g. "plumber near [city]", "HVAC [city]", "med spa
[city]", "auto detailing [city]". Collect businesses that **(a)** have a phone
number, **(b)** under ~50 reviews (smaller = more reachable), **(c)** a basic
or no website. Log name / phone / email / IG into a sheet. 10–20/day.

## The offer (one sentence)
"I set up an automation that instantly texts back any call your business
misses — so you stop losing jobs to whoever the customer calls next. Flat
setup fee, you only pay your own texting (~pennies)."

## Cold email
> **Subject:** quick one about [Business]'s missed calls
>
> Hi [Name] — I'll keep this short. Most [trade] businesses miss 30%+ of calls
> when they're on a job, and those callers just dial the next company.
>
> I set up a simple automation that instantly texts the caller back ("Sorry we
> missed you — how can we help?") so the lead stays with you. Takes a day, flat
> fee, and you only pay a few cents per text.
>
> Want me to send a 30-sec video of how it works for [Business]?
>
> — [You]

## Instagram / FB DM (shorter)
> Hey [Business]! Quick idea — I can set you up so any missed call auto-texts the
> caller back in seconds (stops lost jobs). 1-day setup, flat fee. Want a 30-sec
> demo?

## Cold-call opener
> "Hi, is this [Business]? Quick question — when you're on a job and a call comes
> in that you can't grab, what happens to that customer? … Right. I set up a thing
> that texts them back automatically so they don't call someone else. Can I text
> you a 30-second demo?"

## Free-audit hook (warmer leads / content inbound)
"Free 10-min automation audit: I'll show you the 3 spots your business is
leaking leads and exactly what to automate. No pitch." → books a call.

## Objection handling
- *"I already answer my calls."* → "Love it — this is only for the ones you
  can't grab on a job. Even 2 saved jobs a month pays for it many times over."
- *"Is it expensive?"* → "Flat one-time setup. The only ongoing cost is your own
  texting — pennies per message."
- *"I'm not techy."* → "That's the point — I set it all up, you do nothing."

## Pricing ladder
$300 starter (missed-call text-back) → $500–$1,500 full (text-back +
speed-to-lead + review automation) → optional $99–$300/mo maintenance.

## Daily routine (≈30 min)
1. Add 10–20 targets to the sheet. 2. Send 10 cold emails + 10 DMs.
3. Reply to responders with the 30-sec demo video (use a batch01 render).
4. Book audits. 5. Log everything in `docs/METRICS.md`.
